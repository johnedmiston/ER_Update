Donate to Cybermissions

This donations page serves as the donations page for all of our Cybermissions websites such as: Cybermissions, Global Christians. New Testament Prayer, and Eternity Radio

donate

Cybermissions is a 501(c)3 religious non-profit incorporated in the state of Virginia and donations to Cybermissions are tax-deductible.

we need three kinds of donors

  • one-time gift donors

    who give a generous one-time gift.

  • regular-gift donors

    who give monthly an amount like $25, $50, $100, $250, e.t.c

  • project donors

    who give to see a particular project through to fruition.

ways to give to our mission

  • checks

    Cybermissions P.O. Box 40 Rhoadesville VA 22542 (USA)

  • venmo

    Send to: @Cybermissions

  • zelle

    To donate by Zelle send to: Cybermissions johned@cybermissions.org

  • paypal

    You can also donate by Paypal using a credit card, or your Paypal account, (see Donate by Paypal button near the top of the page), you can also set this as a recurring monthly donation.

  • credit card

    If you wish to donate by credit card then send us an email and we will send you the form that you will need to fill in and send back to us.
